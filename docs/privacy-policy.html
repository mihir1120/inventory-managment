Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ashokvatika handles information in the Ashokvatika Android app.

1. What the app does

Ashokvatika is an inventory management app used to record product details, stock quantities, vendor details, dates, and optional product images.

2. Information handled by the app

  • Product names and inventory counts such as batch, sale, and rent values
  • Vendor details you enter, such as name, phone number, email, address, and website
  • Dates connected with inventory records
  • Optional product images that you capture or select
  • Login entries typed into the app interface

3. How information is used

The app uses this information only to provide its inventory management features on your device.

4. Storage and transmission

Inventory records are stored locally on your device in the app's private database. The app does not transmit your inventory data, vendor data, login entries, or photos to the developer's servers.

The app does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or third-party tracking technologies.

5. Camera, photos, and documents

The app requests camera access only when you choose to capture a product image. The app accesses images or documents only when you choose to select them from your device or from a document provider such as Google Drive.

6. Sharing

If you use the share feature, the app shares only the information you choose through Android's system share sheet to the destination you select. This sharing happens only when you initiate it.

7. Data sharing and sale

Ashokvatika does not sell personal data. Ashokvatika does not share personal data with third parties except when you explicitly initiate sharing from the app.

8. Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you edit it, remove it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. If you selected images from another app or cloud provider, those files remain subject to that provider's own storage and retention practices.

9. Security

The app stores inventory data locally in the Android app sandbox. While reasonable steps are taken to keep data local to your device, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's privacy

This app is not designed specifically for children.

11. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app features, legal requirements, or data practices change.
